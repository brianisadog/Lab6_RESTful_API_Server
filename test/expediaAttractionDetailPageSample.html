      Between the bright lights of Times Square and the sky-high observation deck of the Empire State Building, New York is packed with world-class museums, interactive attractions, and cultural gems. Take your pick of the best with a pass that lets you experience the Big Apple and save while doing it.

      Whether you're visiting New York for a day or a week, there's no shortage of ways to fill your time in the City that Never Sleeps. Plus, your flexible Explorer Pass is valid for a whole month. Art lovers go gaga at the Whitney, MoMA, and the Guggenheim, history buffs of all ages delight in the exhibits of the American Museum of Natural History, and can't-miss landmarks—from the Statue of Liberty to Yankee Stadium—are even more impressive in real life.

      Choose from more than 40 of the city's top sights, crafting an itinerary that suits your schedule and interests, and then grab your camera and your walking shoes to start exploring. With your Explorer Pass and digital guidebook in hand, the city's historic monuments, cultural treasures, and vibrant neighborhoods are at your fingertips. In addition to saving on the price of admission, your pass also grants you special discounts at local retailers and restaurants.

      Highlights

      • Admission to your choice of NYC's attractions & museums

      • More than 40 museums, tours & attractions to choose from

      • Explorer Pass Guidebook for easily navigating the city

      • Landmarks like Empire State Building, MoMA & Ellis Island

      • Savings of up to 45 percent off retail admission prices

      Inclusions

      • Explorer Pass valid for admission to selected tours, museums, and attractions

      • Explorer Pass Guidebook and map in English

      • Discounts at local shops and restaurants

      Know Before You Book

      • Children 2 and younger are complimentary at most attractions.

      • Advance reservations may be required for certain attractions; mention that you are a New York City Explorer Pass holder.

      • Your Explorer Pass is valid for 30 days from first use.

      • The Shearwater Classic Daytime Schooner Sail is available seasonally from May–September. Please check days and hours of operation.

      • The Clipper City Tall Ship Sail is available seasonally from May–September. Please check days and hours of operation.

      • BEAST Speedboat Ride: Circle Line Sightseeing is available seasonally from May–September. Please check days and hours of operation.

      • Luna Park at Coney Island is available seasonally from April–October. Please check days and hours of operation.

      • Days and hours of operation for each attraction vary; please check before you go.
